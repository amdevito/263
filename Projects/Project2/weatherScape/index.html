Choose a city anywhere in the world and press 'SUBMIT'. The AnyDay Music Machine will compose a generative synth composition based on the current weather in that location. The sound piece will vary in tonality, matching the weather to the tonal colour - light and happy (clear skies or some clouds) to sad (rain), to dark (heavy overcast, thunderstorm) and evil (fog, mist), using MODES. Explore the weather and thus different soundscapes in different cities around the world. Close this dialog using the [x] and access this menu again by clicking the ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ button. The animated rippling title is movable for your convenience.
